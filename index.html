Game Rules and Instructions

Welcome to Takashi: The Samurai Duel Game!

  • Player 1 uses 'X' and Player 2 (or AI) uses 'O'.
  • Take turns to place your symbol on the board.
  • The first player to align three symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.
  • Music will play when you win, lose, or draw.
  • Use the controls to reset the game or toggle AI mode.
